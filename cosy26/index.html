Canadian Operator Symposium

June 1 to June 5, 2026

Carleton University, Ottawa


The 53rd anniversary of the Canadian Operator Symposium (COSy) will be held at Carleton University, on the unceded territory of the Algonquin Anishinaabe nation. We will take the opportunity at this COSy to celebrate the eightieth birthday of George Elliott. More details will come soon!

Organized by Jason Crann, Matthias Neufang, Charles Starling, and Aaron Tikuisis.


With support from the Fields Institute.
