Summer School in Operator Algebras - Travel and accommodation

June 9-12, 2020

University of Ottawa, Canada


Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, this summer school is indefinitely postponed.

Travelling to Ottawa

If you intend to fly to Ottawa, the airport code is YOW, and there is a 10% discount available if flying on Air Canada: see here. Getting from the airport to the University of Ottawa by public transportation takes 30-45 minutes, and involves taking route 97 to Hurdman station (last stop) and then taking the light rail train west (towards Tunney's Pasture) for 2 stops, to uOttawa. A taxi from the airport to the University of Ottawa costs around $30. You can also reach Ottawa by Via rail. To get from the train station to the University of Ottawa by public transportation takes 10 minutes; take the light rail train west (towards Tunney's Pasture) for 3 stops, to uOttawa.

Accommodation

Participants who apply for support will likely obtain support in the form of accommodation. This accommodation will be in the University residence. The University of Ottawa is essentially located in downtown Ottawa (on the East side), and there are many hotels available.

Supported by the Fields Institute and the National Science Foundation.
