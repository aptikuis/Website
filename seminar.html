The Ottawa Analysis Seminar

The Ottawa Analysis Seminar is run by the analysis groups at uOttawa and Carleton. Weekly seminars are held during term time, generally alternating between the two universities.

Faculty in the analysis seminar include: Jason Crann, Ken Davidson, Thierry Giordano Matthias Neufang, Charles Starling, Aaron Tikuisis.

Please contact Aaron Tikuisis to be included on the seminar mailing list.
